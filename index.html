Hi, I'm Michael Batavia

I am a junior at the NYU Tandon School of Engineering and have done a whole bunch of engineering projects and service projects to further my dream of being the best and greatest AI engineer you have ever seen.

In pursuit of these goals, I have worked in a research lab with Dr. John Ross Rizzo of NYU Langone to develop a haptic feedback headset for the blind in NYC, become the executive director of the non-profit TeenHacksLI to raise awareness of computer science projects and development in Long Island and won awards for my app development and machine learning projects focusing on preventing the spread of COVID-19 during the COVID-19 pandemic and to pre-diagnose breast cancer from slides of metastatic lymph nodes. But that's enough about that, you should explore the page and find out about my silly exploits for yourself!
